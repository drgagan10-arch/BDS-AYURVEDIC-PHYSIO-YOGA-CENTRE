About the Doctors

Meet Our Experts at BDS Center, Amritsar

Dr. Gagandeep Singh

BAMS, PhD – Ayurvedic Consultant

Dr. Gagandeep Singh specializes in Ayurvedic treatments for chronic conditions, liver & kidney disorders, arthritis, diabetes, and holistic wellness. With years of experience, he combines traditional Ayurveda with modern healthcare knowledge to deliver personalized care to every patient.

Dr. Kirti

Physiotherapy & Yoga Specialist

Dr. Kirti leads the Physiotherapy and Yoga departments at BDS Center. She focuses on rehabilitation for back pain, cervical, knee, and joint problems, as well as stress management and flexibility improvement through yoga therapy. Her approach ensures a balance between physical recovery and mental wellness.

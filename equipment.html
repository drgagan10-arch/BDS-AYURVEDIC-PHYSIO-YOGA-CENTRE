⚡ Advanced Physiotherapy Equipment

State‑of‑the‑art technology for faster recovery, pain relief & rehabilitation

At BDS Ayurvedic Physio & Yoga Centre, we use world‑class physiotherapy machines and tools to treat orthopedic conditions, sports injuries, neurological issues, and chronic pain. Our integrated approach blends evidence‑based electrotherapy, therapeutic exercise, and modern rehabilitation devices.
Electrotherapy

TENS & NMES

Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation blocks pain signals, while Neuromuscular Electrical Stimulation re‑educates muscles, reduces spasms, and improves local blood flow.

Deep Healing

Therapeutic Ultrasound

High‑frequency sound waves penetrate deep into tissues, accelerating soft tissue healing, reducing inflammation, and relieving chronic tendonitis.

Strength & Gait

Parallel Bars & Treadmill

Medical‑grade parallel bars for balance training, plus antigravity treadmill for stroke recovery, gait retraining and post‑surgical ambulation.

Cardio Rehab

Motorized Cycle Ergometer

Passive + active leg exercise bike to improve joint mobility, prevent muscle atrophy, and enhance cardiovascular endurance for bedridden or weak patients.

Thermal Modality

Shortwave Diathermy (SWD)

Deep heating of soft tissues increases extensibility, reduces joint stiffness, and soothes chronic back pain, osteoarthritis and muscle spasms.

✔️ Why our physiotherapy equipment stands out: Every machine – from interferential therapy (IFT) units to cervical traction devices – is regularly calibrated and used under expert supervision. We blend traditional hands‑on therapy with modern medical technology to restore function, reduce pain, and improve quality of life. Dr. Kirti (Physiotherapy Specialist) customizes protocols using tools like the shoulder pulley, foam rollers, and balance boards to achieve optimal outcomes.

Conditions treated: Cervical & Lumbar Spondylosis, Knee Osteoarthritis, Paralysis rehabilitation, Sports Injuries, Post‑fracture stiffness, and more.

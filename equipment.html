State‑of‑the‑art technology for faster recovery, pain relief & rehabilitation

At BDS Ayurvedic Physio & Yoga Centre, we use world‑class physiotherapy machines and tools to treat orthopedic conditions, sports injuries, neurological issues, and chronic pain.
Electrotherapy

TENS & NMES

Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation blocks pain signals, while Neuromuscular Electrical Stimulation re‑educates muscles.

Therapeutic Ultrasound

High‑frequency sound waves accelerate soft tissue healing.

Deep heating to reduce joint stiffness and soothe chronic pain.
